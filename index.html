Best Sites

Below you will find the best sites to help you conquer the game. These sites are the communities' "Go-To" for loadouts, trophy hunting, prepping for new seasons and getting your Fireteam together. I have been playing Destiny since the Alpha stages of the game and the below sites have become essential to me, I hope they can help you enjoy the game and all it has to offer.

Braytech

Braytech is a Destiny fan site that allows users to view and map checklists, track and view triumphs, inspect collectibles, and so much more. A big part of Destiny is "Seals", Seals are groups of Destiny 2 triumphs that once fully completed awards the player with a Title, that can be shown in-game under the player's name. Braytech allows you to easily track the triumphs you require to unlock those titles. Some triumphs require the collection of many items, with Braytech it makes it a breeze as you can tell at anytime which collectible you are missing.

Visit Braytech

Destiny Item Manager

Destiny Item Manager is a must for any Destiny player who needs to manage their loadouts and vaults on the fly. Forgot to grab your favorite hand cannon from the vault? Just click and drag it to your active character while flying into your activity. Equip it directly if you're in the tower or hanging out in orbit! Use DIM's powerful loadout optimizer to choose the stats and mods that you want and let DIM find the perfect set of items to get you there. DIM can even equip your mods for you when you create a loadout that specifies them! Many Youtubers create character builds and they use DIM to create and share their loadouts, it may be the most important site for any Guardian.

Visit DIM

Destiny Recipes

Destiny Recipes is the best site to get yourself prepped for a new season. It has a fantastic PRE-SEASONAL CHECKLIST which will help you prep efficiently for the new season. It will advise what are the best bounties to store so you can turn them in at the start of the season for some quick xp. There is also a Materials & Resources checklist which advises what you currently have and how many you need to max out your inventory. There is also a TARGETED WEAPONS section that advises what weapons will be leaving and suggests weapons you should pick up.

Visit Destiny Recipes

Reddit

Reddit and specifically "DestinyTheGame" reddit is a fantastic place to find out all the news and secrets within the game. The "DestinyTheGame" reddit community has grown to a whopping 2.6 million Guardians over the years and is a destination you will find yourself going to daily. On the "DestinyTheGame" reddit you will find the latest guides, character builds, new expansion news and so much more. There are many other Destiny subs too like "raidsecrets" which is a dedicated sub to finding all the secrets that the Raids in Destiny have to offer, also there is "Fireteams" which allows you to link up with other Guardians to play the game together.

Visit Reddit

Best Apps

Below you will find the best apps to help you repel the darkness. The below mobile apps are fan favorites and all have their advantages whether you want to move weapons, join a fireteam, check someone's stats or just see what weapons Xur (Destiny Weapon/Armor Merchant) is selling this week you will have access to all this information right on your phone.

Destiny Companion

The official Destiny 2 Companion app keeps you connected to your Destiny adventure wherever life takes you. See the latest featured content. Track your progress towards bounties, quests, and challenges. Discover what events and activities are live in the game. See your current rank for seasonal rewards, acquire bounties, claim rewards from the previous season, and inspect vendors for what they have available. The app also comes with a built in LFG (looking for a group.) so you can join players in-game.

Destiny Trials Report

Destiny Trials Report is the ultimate companion for players of Trials of Osiris, a game mode in Bungie's video game Destiny 2. While loading your next match you can search for your opponents to see valuable information on their style of play and skills. Among other things you see their loadout, kill/death-ratio, and how they did so far in the current week. Reach out to us through @TrialsReport on Twitter if you have any feedback, questions or ideas!
